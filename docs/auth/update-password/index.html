Update Your Password

To update your password, please use the reset link sent to your email. That link will bring you to a secure form where you can set a new password.

If you haven't requested a reset link yet, open the BOUNTYExpo app and tap "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in screen.

Open App

How it works:

1. Open the BOUNTYExpo app and tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email and tap "Send Reset Link"
3. Check your email and click the reset link
4. The link opens a secure page where you set a new password
